# Privacy Policy Draft

This app stores vocabulary words, review history, goals, reminders, and optional AI study content locally on the device.

## Data the app stores

- Vocabulary words you add or import
- Review progress, streaks, and scheduling history
- Reminder preferences
- Optional AI study-kit responses cached for faster reuse

## Network requests

The app may contact third-party services when you use these features:

- Dictionary lookup and word import
- Optional AI study-kit generation if you configure an LLM endpoint

The app does not require an account and does not include built-in analytics or advertising.

## Data sharing

- Your study data stays on-device unless you explicitly export a backup file or configure your own backend.
- If you use AI features through a proxy or direct API key, the word content you request is sent to that endpoint.

## Backups

- Backup export is user-initiated.
- Backup files are shared only through the platform share sheet or imported manually by the user.

## Contact / publishing note

Before submitting to the App Store or Play Store, replace this draft with your final legal/privacy-policy URL and update it to match any analytics, crash reporting, auth, or backend services you add later.
